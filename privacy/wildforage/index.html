Privacy Policy

Wild Foraging Guide Pro
Last updated: March 30, 2026

A&J Tech ("we," "our," or "us") built the Wild Foraging Guide Pro app as a reference guide for identifying edible wild plants and mushrooms in North America. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Wild Foraging Guide Pro is designed to function primarily as an offline reference tool. We collect minimal personal information:

  • Device information: We may collect non-personally identifiable device data (e.g., device type, operating system version) to help us improve app stability.
  • Usage analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics to understand which features are used most frequently.
  • Advertising data: Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with Google's privacy policies.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

2. How We Use Information

  • To maintain and improve app performance and stability
  • To serve advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience

3. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob — for serving in-app advertisements. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Firebase Analytics — for crash reporting and usage analytics. Data is anonymized and aggregated.

4. Data Retention

We retain aggregated, anonymized analytics data for up to 12 months. We do not store personally identifiable information on our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.
